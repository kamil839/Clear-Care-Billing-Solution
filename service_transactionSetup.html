ELECTRONIC TRANSACTONS

electronic-transactions-Official paperwork is the bane of our lives. After a long day, the last thing you need is a stack of forms to fill out. A considerable proportion of situations involving payment delays and claim denials are the result of incorrect enrollment. When we onboard your practice, we ensure that your EDI, ERA, and EFT are properly configure.


electronic-transactions-We assign a team of professionals who are experience in filling out the forms and applications. Though at the outset it may look like a simple task, it requires a sharp eye for detail as a missing number can cause significant delays in the reimbursements.



HOW WE WORK

1. EDI Enrollment

Depending upon the clearing house and insurance company, sometimes EDI enrollment is required to submit the claims electronically. Basically, it’s the process to let the insurance company know that provider will be using this clearinghouse to submit the claims. It smooths the path of your medical claim to the payer’s system and ensures that your claim hits the payer system in the first go and reimbursements are not delayed.

2. ERA Enrollment

ERA is the electronic form of EOB and can be enrolled to gain faster results of your claims submissions. It is receive directly in the clearinghouse and reduces the hassle of paper EOBs delivered to the provider’s mailing address. Inaccurate enrollment setup might result in a huge workload and delays when you manually go through all paper checks and EOBs (Explanation of Benefits).

3. EFT Enrollment

EFT is simply the electronic transfer of payments from an insurance company to a provider. EFTs never go through clearinghouses. We make every effort to keep your medical practice as paperless as feasible. This guarantees that all of your electronic fund transfers run smoothly and that the difficulties of dealing with paper checks and EOBs avoid. You get your money much faster, and the billing team is in an accounting heaven.
